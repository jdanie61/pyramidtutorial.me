PYRAMID at a Glance

Specialities Pyramid Learning Center

Faculty – Our Biggest Strength

The core strength of academics is the Faculty. At Pyramid, we believe in maintaining an excellent quality education on behalf of highly experienced and qualified IITian based faculties for JEE and Medical domain for NEET. These Faculties take continuous follow-up of students apart from regular classroom sessions. This ensures the quality results year after year
Learn more

1000+ Title Library

In addition to the exhaustive Study Material, Pyramid provides access to various renowned national / international books, periodicals and journals.
Learn more

Digital Attendance

Biometric / Electronic Identity cards with batch and registration number are issued to each admitted student. Attendance is also taken through Biometric recorder. Every student is required to show thumb impression on the electronic gadget while entering into the institute premises.
Learn more

Modern Classrooms

Our academic classrooms are most modern, having taste full interiors, ventilation and lighting for conductive and motivational learning environment.
Learn more

Monthly Progress Report

Regular M.P.R. with comparison graph of success helps everyone to track the student’s success ratio and immediate improvements. We provide all updates to students and parents via SMS, Whatsapp, and Physical Mark sheet. Pyramid App shows M.P.R. in real time to analyze and rectify time to time.
Learn more

Rigorous Individual – Doubt Solving Sessions

Our teachers are always available to all students for individual doubt-solving thereby enabling the teacher to adapt a methodology best suited to any specific child. This helps the teacher to further monitor the child’s progress, making sure what is taught is understood correctly.
